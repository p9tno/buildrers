Smooth Plaster vs. Shimmering Pebble: Which Swimming Pool Finish is Right for You?

Both smooth plaster and shimmering pebble are viable options for swimming pool finishes. Smooth plaster is an affordable option with a clean and simple appearance, while shimmering pebble is a more durable option that adds a luxurious touch to the pool. The decision ultimately depends on personal preference, budget, and maintenance requirements.

Smooth Plaster Finish

Pros

  • Smooth plaster finish is an affordable option for swimming pool finishes.
  • This finish is easy to install, and the application process is simple.
  • It is a customizable option, and you can choose from various colors to achieve the desired look.
  • The smooth finish feels nice to the touch and can be more comfortable for swimmers.

Cons

  • The cost of a smooth plaster finish is relatively low compared to other options, with an average cost of $5 to $7 per square foot.

Maintenance

  • Smooth plaster requires frequent maintenance, including regular brushing and acid washing to remove stains and prevent discoloration. The maintenance cost can vary depending on the size of the pool and the frequency of the maintenance.

Visual Appeal:

  • Smooth plaster has a clean and simple look, and it is a popular option for those who prefer a classic, timeless appearance. The finish can be customized to achieve the desired color and texture.
